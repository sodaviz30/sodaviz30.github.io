Here you can see that xxx is most likely to happen in DISTRICT AT SOME TIME. Assault and motor vehicle theft generally have higher rates at night, with an increase Friday and Saturday evening/night. This makes sense, as perpetrators prefer being as hidden as possible. The increase also makes sense as people are more likely to go out on weekends, which increases the victim pool. One safety measure to take is to steer clear of these areas in the evening, and if that is not possible to stay in well lit areas whenever possible.
Burglary also increases on Friday evenings, again since people are more likely to go out, leaving more homes unattended. Here one could make it look like, the house is still occupied, for example by drawing the blinds and leaving some light on.
